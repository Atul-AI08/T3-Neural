About T3 Neural

Welcome to T3 Neural, a modern twist on the timeless game of Tic Tac Toe!

At T3 Neural, we've brought together the simplicity of a classic game and the sophistication of cutting-edge artificial intelligence to deliver an engaging and intelligent gaming experience. The game isn't just about Xs and Os anymore - it's about strategy, learning, and innovation.

How It Works

AI-Powered Gameplay:

The heart of T3 Neural lies in its AI bot, meticulously trained using Q-Learning, a reinforcement learning algorithm. This ensures the bot continuously adapts and learns optimal strategies, making it a challenging and unpredictable opponent for players of all skill levels.

Backend Excellence:

T3 Neural's backend is powered by FastAPI, providing a fast, reliable, and efficient server to handle all AI computations and game logic seamlessly.

Dynamic Frontend:

The user interface is crafted using Angular, combining a sleek, responsive design with smooth, interactive gameplay.
